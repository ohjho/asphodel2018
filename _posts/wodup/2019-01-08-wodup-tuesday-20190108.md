<h3>A.  For Time: Running, Rowing, Assault Bike, and Double-Unders</h3>


In teams of 2:

For Time:<br/>– Run 1200 meters<br/>– 100 Calories of Rowing<br/>– Run 800 meters<br/>– 60 Calories of Assault Bike<br/>– Run 400 meters<br/>– 200 Double-Unders<br/><br/>The run and calories can be shared between partners. You go, I go.

200 Double Unders must be completed by each team member. You go, I go.<em>Rest 5 minutes</em>
<h3>B.  5 minute AMRAP of Deadlifts and Single Arm Dumbbell Push Presses</h3>
As Many Rounds and Reps as possible in 5 minutes:<br/>– 3 Deadlifts @ 125/84 kg<br/>– 7 Single Arm Dumbbell Push Presses @ 25/17.5 kg<br/><br/>You must complete all 7 reps on one arm before switching arms.
